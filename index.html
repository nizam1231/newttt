- Here, we will explain to you the project idea,
how it started, and what TLB is.

The project owner needed an idea to support the private artisan sector, especially in furniture. He desired a qualitative leap that was unlike any before; he wanted distinction and uniqueness. He needed a project that would launch from the city of Tripoli, Lebanon, to the entire world. He wanted to show the world the beauty and creativity of a city whose artisanal works have flourished and continue to flourish with ingenuity to this day.

Then, he began the preparations... He started working on eight sheets of paper, embarking on the journey of a million steps with his modest progress, step by step. I wanted the furniture design to reflect the city's cultural and historical heritage, incorporating Scandinavian touches and techniques. I began drawing and learning on seven A4 sheets, and the eighth is what you see now. So this is where the idea began. A piece of furniture was designed to be the start of the TLB project. To make it unique and different, and to reach a wider audience, the project owner decided to incorporate NFTs into this work. The design was published on the "OpenSea" platform with the seven documents under the name TheFirst Design.
